Tank Maturity Score

Every tank earns a 0–100 maturity score based on three pillars of a healthy aquarium: age, water stability, and livestock diversity. Watch your score grow from New to Mature as you build a thriving ecosystem.

Points increase with tank age — from 0 at day one to 30 at two years. Patience pays off.

The largest component. Lower coefficient of variation across key parameters over 60 days = higher score. Consistent chemistry is king.

Up and Running in 3 Commands

Docker makes deployment effortless. Clone, configure, launch.

Terminal
$ git clone https://github.com/eprifti/AquaScope.git
$ cd AquaScope && cp .env.example .env
$ docker compose up -d
✓ Backend ready at http://localhost:8000
✓ Frontend ready at http://localhost
✓ 4 containers running (app, db, influxdb, nginx)

That's it. Open http://localhost and start tracking your aquarium.
